Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026  ·  Effective: 21 April 2026

1. Who We Are

Eastlink Media ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller responsible for your personal data. We are a digital agency providing ecommerce growth, affiliate marketing, SEO, GEO, and creative digital services.

Contact: hello@eastlinkmedia.com

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We only collect personal data that you actively provide to us through the contact form on our website.

Data Purpose Legal Basis
Name To address you in our reply Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR)
Email address To respond to your enquiry Legitimate interests / pre-contractual steps
Service interest To direct your enquiry to the right team Legitimate interests
Message content To understand and respond to your enquiry Legitimate interests / pre-contractual steps

We do not collect sensitive personal data, payment card data, or data relating to children.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your business enquiry
  • Discuss and fulfil any subsequent engagement with you
  • Maintain a record of our business correspondence

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling, and we do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your data with the following third-party processors strictly to operate our services:

Processor Purpose Location
Resend Email delivery of contact form submissions USA (standard contractual clauses)
Cloudflare Website infrastructure, CDN, DDoS protection Global (adequacy / SCCs)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain contact enquiry data for up to 2 years from the date of receipt. Data is deleted sooner if you request erasure (see Section 7) or when retention is no longer necessary for the original purpose.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies on this website.

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) and your cookie consent choice. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-party CDN assets

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts and icons from Font Awesome via CDN. When your browser requests these assets, your IP address is transmitted to those providers. We have no control over any data those providers may process. Choosing "Essential Only" in our cookie banner does not prevent these CDN requests, as they are required to render the site correctly.

If you prefer not to load third-party CDN assets, you may use a browser extension to block them.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights in relation to personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you (Article 15)
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data (Article 16)
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten") (Article 17)
  • Right to restriction — ask us to restrict how we use your data (Article 18)
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format (Article 20)
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests (Article 21)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@eastlinkmedia.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Right to Complain

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK supervisory authority for data protection:

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Phone: 0303 123 1113

We would, however, appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns directly before you contact the ICO.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Our website is served exclusively over HTTPS. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely to our email delivery provider (Resend) and are not stored on our web server.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

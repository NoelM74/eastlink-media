How AI engines decide which brands to cite
01

Entity Authority

AI engines understand the world through entities — brands, people, places, concepts. We engineer your brand as a clearly-defined, well-cited entity in the knowledge graph, so AI models know exactly what you are, what you do, and why you're authoritative.

02

AI-Crawlable Content Architecture

AI models are trained on and crawl structured, authoritative content. We restructure your site so every page speaks the language of AI retrieval: clean semantic HTML, schema markup, FAQ structures, and citation-worthy factual statements that LLMs can surface verbatim.

03

Citation Signal Network

Being cited in AI outputs requires being cited across the web — in reputable publications, forums, industry databases, and partner sites. We build the off-page citation ecosystem that tells AI engines your brand is the authoritative source on your topics.

04

Technical Foundation

A slow, technically broken site cannot earn AI authority — period. Core Web Vitals, crawlability, structured data, and page speed are the non-negotiable infrastructure layer that every GEO strategy is built on.
